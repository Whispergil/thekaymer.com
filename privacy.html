Privacy Policy

Kaymer LLC — thekaymer.com

Last updated: June 26, 2025

Kaymer LLC ("we," "us," or "our") operates the website thekaymer.com. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle information when you visit our website.

1. Information We Collect

We do not actively collect personal information from visitors to thekaymer.com unless you voluntarily provide it through our contact form.

When you submit our contact form, we collect:

  • Your first and last name
  • Your email address
  • Your message and selected topic

This information is sent directly to our email inbox and is used solely to respond to your inquiry.

2. Automatically Collected Information

Our website is hosted by Vercel, Inc. Vercel may automatically collect certain technical information when you visit our site, including:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Pages visited and time spent
  • Referring website

This data is collected by Vercel for hosting and performance purposes. For more information, please review Vercel's Privacy Policy.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use the information you provide through our contact form only to:

  • Respond to your questions or inquiries
  • Provide support related to our apps

We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes.

4. Cookies

We do not use cookies on thekaymer.com. Vercel may use cookies for essential hosting and performance functions. You can control cookies through your browser settings.

5. Data Retention

Contact form submissions are retained in our email inbox only as long as necessary to respond to your inquiry. We do not store form submissions in any database.

6. Children's Privacy

Our website is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe we have inadvertently collected information from a child, please contact us immediately.

7. Links to Other Websites

Our website may contain links to third-party websites. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those sites and encourage you to review their privacy policies.

8. Your Rights

You have the right to request access to, correction of, or deletion of any personal information you have provided to us. To make such a request, please contact us at the email below.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by updating the date at the top of this page. Continued use of our website after any changes constitutes your acceptance of the updated policy.
